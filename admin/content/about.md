## Our Mission

Catechism for Africa exists to support the evangelizing mission of the Catholic Church across the African continent. We believe that every Catholic—whether in a bustling city parish or a remote village chapel—deserves access to high-quality, doctrinally sound catechetical formation.

Our platform bridges the gap between the universal teaching of the Church and the particular contexts of African faith communities. We produce video content, study materials, and teaching resources that are faithful to the Magisterium while speaking to the hearts and minds of African Catholics.

## Our Story

Founded in response to the growing need for accessible catechetical materials in Africa, Catechism for Africa began as a small project serving a handful of parishes. Today, we partner with dioceses across eight countries, providing formation resources that reach thousands of Catholics.

Our team includes theologians, catechists, educators, and media professionals—all committed to the New Evangelization and the formation of missionary disciples on the continent Pope Francis has called "the lungs of humanity."

## Our Approach

We follow a three-pillar approach to catechetical content: fidelity to the Church's teaching, relevance to African lived experience, and accessibility through modern technology. Every video lesson is reviewed for doctrinal accuracy, culturally contextualized, and optimized for delivery on low-bandwidth connections.
